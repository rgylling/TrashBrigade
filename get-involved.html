Get involved

"Find out what you can do to help La Brigada"

There are many ways you can help out La Brigada. We always need more adult supervisors - spanish language skills are helpful but not neccessary. You are more than welcome to come with us when we clean the streets and help us keep an eye on the kids. Donations of Toys, Trash Bags, Candy, Gloves etc. are more than welcome. We usually start our Christmas Toy Drive around Thanksgiving time. We'd love for you to bring in a toy, unwrapped, for either a boy or a girl toddler to teenager. Of course cash donations are more than welcome as well. Just come on by any Saturday morning and meet the wonderful children of La Brigada de la Basura. We would all love to see you!
