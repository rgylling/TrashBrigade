About

"Working for a better future"

In 2010 Michael and Yvonne Hayes upon walking around the streets of their neighborhood were saddened and disgusted by the amounts of trash lying around everywhere. Apart from simply being unhygienic the piles of trash are also a breeding ground for the mosquitos carrying the dangerous Dengue virus. So they decided to bring to life La Brigada de la Basura - The Trash Brigade. Every Saturday local children are given trash bags and latex gloves; then with adult supervision they walk through the local streets picking up every piece of trash they can find. We strive to keep educating the kids about the environment and the importance of protecting it. We hope to teach the children responsibility, the importance of cleaning up after yourself and also hope to promote inter-cultural communication between the locals and visitors.
